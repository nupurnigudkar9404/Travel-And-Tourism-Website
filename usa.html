Days Plan

  • Day 1

    New York/New Jersey


    Arrive in New York. Report to Coach And Proceed to Hotel.
  • Day 2

    New York/New Jersey


    City orientation tour includes the United Nations Building, Rockefeller Center and 5th Avenue, Brooklyn Bridge, Manhatten Bridge, Trump Tower, wall street, charging bull, pass by Central Park and Ground Zero Point - the site of the World Trade Center. Travel by ferry to visit the Statue of Liberty on Liberty island. Free time for shopping . Later visit One World Tower Observatory Deck – the tallest sky scrapper in the western hemisphere to enjoy stunning views of New York city. Enjoy drive along the famous Time Square & Manhattan city
  • Day 3

    New York--Washington


    Proceed to Washington. Arrive at Washington. Visit to the Lincoln Memorial followed by the orientation city tour which includes the Union Railway Station, Capitol Hill and Pentagon building. Also pass by Washington Monument. Photograph yourself at the background of the White House. Later visit the Smithsonian Air and Space Museum .
  • Day 4

    Washington--Niagara


    Proceed to Niagara. Enroute, visit Hershey's chocolate world, visitors will have the opportunity to learn how chocolate is made and even try the delicious food and desserts. Arrive in Niagara.
  • Day 5

    Niagara


    Today visit the most awaited attraction of the tour - Niagara Falls. Get an experience of a lifetime as you enjoy Helicopter Ride over Niagara Falls to get spectacular views of the Horseshoe Falls, Canadian side of Niagara Falls and the Niagara river. Later visit the Niagara Aerospace Museum is an aviation museum. Visit the Goat island, experience the thrill of Cave of the Winds (Subject to Operation). Later visit Niagara Falls Observation Tower to get the spectacular view of both American and Horseshoe falls at the same time, then board the 'Maid of the Mist' Cruise to take a close encounter with the falls( Subject to Operation). Explore the Niagara falls illuminations in the evening.
  • Day 6

    Niagara--Chicago


    Board the flight to Chicago. Arrival in Chicago. On arrival we enjoy Chicago Architecture boat tour followed by city tour which includes, Swami Vivekananda way, Buckingham fountain, Claude Gate – The bean, Michigan Avenue, later visit Lake Michigan water front.
  • Day 7

    Chicago--Las Vegas


    Take flight to Las Vegas. Arrive in Las Vegas. Visit some of the best hotels known for their interiors like Luxor, Venetian, etc. Visit to Fremont area in the evening.
  • Day 8

    Las Vegas--Hoover Dam--Grand Canyon


    Early morning enjoy View to Hoover Dam & Lake Mead, the largest water reservoir in US. Proceed to view Grand Canyon by Skywalk takes you over the edge on the first-ever 4,000-foot high glass bridge.Later once again see various hotels known for Casinos and shows and enjoy 'V' Show – The Ultimate Variety Show. In the evening experience the newest attraction in Las Vegas – The High Roller, The World's Tallest Observation wheel for an Illuminated view of Las Vegas.
  • Day 9

    Las Vegas--Los Angeles


    Proceed to Los Angeles. Arrive in Los Angeles – the world famous city of Hollywood stars. View Beverly Hills Area, Hollywood Drive and Sunset Boulevard. Photo-stop at Dolby Theatre. Later enjoy the Hollywood Walk of Fame is a historic landmark which consists of more than 2,765 five-pointed terrazzo-and-brass stars embedded in the sidewalks.
