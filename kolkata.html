Kolkata:Victoria Memorial,Dakshineswar Kali Temple,Sundarbans

Days Plan

  • Day 1

    Kolkata


    Start at Victoria Memorial: Begin your day with a visit to this iconic monument. Explore the museum inside, which houses various exhibits related to India's history. St. Paul's Cathedral: Located near Victoria Memorial, visit this beautiful cathedral with its striking architecture.
  • Day 2

    Howrah Bridge


    Indian Museum: Spend your afternoon exploring the Indian Museum, one of the oldest and largest museums in India, showcasing an extensive collection of artifacts. Howrah Bridge: Take a stroll along the Howrah Bridge, an iconic symbol of Kolkata.
  • Day 3

    Dakshineswar Kali Temple


    Start your day with a visit to this famous Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Kali, situated on the banks of the Hooghly River. Sample some street food at Dakshineswar or opt for a nearby restaurant. Kalighat Kali Temple: Visit another famous Kali Temple, Kalighat Kali Temple, which holds significant religious importance. Jorasanko Thakur Bari (Tagore House): Explore the ancestral home of the Tagore family, now converted into a museum displaying Rabindranath Tagore's life and works.Explore the Academy of Fine Arts, a hub for cultural events and exhibitions, showcasing works of renowned artists.Conclude your day with a stroll along Park Street, lined with restaurants, cafes, and shops. You can also enjoy the vibrant nightlife here.
  • Day 4

    Botanical Gardens


    Spend your afternoon at the Botanical Gardens, home to a vast collection of plants and trees, including the iconic Great Banyan Tree. Science City: Explore Science City, an interactive science center with various exhibits and activities suitable for all ages.
  • Day 5

    Rabindra Sarobar:


    Start your day with a visit to the Academy of Fine Arts, a cultural institution showcasing works of art by renowned artists. Explore its galleries and attend any ongoing exhibitions. Rabindra Sarobar: Enjoy a morning walk or leisurely boat ride at Rabindra Sarobar, a tranquil lake surrounded by lush greenery. Treat your taste buds to the flavors of Bengali cuisine at a traditional restaurant. Marble Palace: Explore the opulent Marble Palace, known for its exquisite architecture, impressive art collection, and lush gardens.
  • Day 5

    Sundarbans


    Embark on a scenic ferry ride across the Hooghly River, offering a unique perspective of Kolkata's waterfront landmarks. Sundarbans Day Trip (Optional): If time permits, consider a day trip to the Sundarbans National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site renowned for its mangrove forests, wildlife, and biodiversity. Return to Kolkata and unwind at your hotel or explore local markets for souvenirs.
  • Day 6

    Park Street


    Explore the opulent Marble Palace, known for its exquisite architecture, impressive art collection, and lush gardens. Park Street: Wander along Park Street, Kolkata's vibrant thoroughfare, dotted with restaurants, cafes, boutiques, and pubs. Indulge in dinner at a local restaurant and soak in the lively ambiance of Park Street.
  • Day 7

    Departure from Kolkata


    Board the flight for your home town. Tour Concludes.
