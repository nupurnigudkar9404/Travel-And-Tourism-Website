Days Plan

  • Day 1

    Arrive in London


    Arrive in London - Vibrant, Bustling, historic, cosmopolitan capital city of United Kingdom. Famous for its monarchy, Big Ben & World famous London Bridge. On arrival set out to see England's architectural treasures on a day trip to Windsor Castle – the weekend home of the Royal family. Step inside to see the opulent chambers.
  • Day 2

    London


    Today visit the Tower of London, UNESCO World Heritage site, it is one of the most secured castle around world, get a chance to see Kohinoor Diamond kept for exhibition. You can also photograph yourself with the 'Yeomen Warders', the ceremonial guardians of the Tower of London. Next visit the famous Madame Tussaud's wax museum. This museum houses a unique collection of world's largest wax works of famous personalities, including India's top leaders and film stars. Then we proceed to enjoy city orientation covering Hyde Park, Big Ben, Westminster Abbey, Piccadilly Circus, River Thames, Houses of Parliament, Trafalgar Square, Tower Bridge. Free time for shopping at the famous Oxford / Bakers Street. (If time permits) Admire panoramic views over the Thames River, Big Ben and other landmarks on this 30 minute flight – The London Eye Ride - Europe’s tallest observation wheel.
  • Day 3

    London--Paris


    Board the highly acclaimed Eurostar to cross the English channel from London to Paris. Arrive at Paris - Fashion Capital of the world, dazzling, shining & Romantic city, famous for high quality gastronomy and world class clothing brands. One of the most beautiful cities in the World. Upon arrival visit the Paris's most famous and awaited attraction of your trip, The Eiffel Tower - World’s most recognizable landmark, Masterpiece of modern architecture. The most enthralling landmark of Paris. Take the elevator and access the top level of Eiffel Tower from where you can get fantastic views of Paris city (Top Level subject to operation/ Available). Paris city orientation tour to see the most famous landmarks. Travel past the Champs Elysses, L’Arc de Triomphe and Place de la Concorde. Enjoy the Seine river cruise in the evening.
  • Day 4

    Disneyland--Paris


    Join an exciting trip to Disneyland®. Get charmed by Cinderella castle. Take an exciting roller coaster ride down the Thunder Mountain. Visit Fantasy land and meet Alice in Wonderland, Pinocchio, Peter Pan and Snow White. Get thrilled by the legends of Aladdin and Indiana Jones at Adventure Ride a space shuttle to the moon at Discovery land. Encounter Ghost Ships, steam trains and western scenes in the Wild West town of Frontier land.
  • Day 5

    Paris--Bruges--Brussels-Amsterdam/Rotterdam


    Today proceed to explore the city of Bruges – famous for lace making, charming cobblestone streets and romantic canals. On arrival enjoy walking tour of this lovely city. Later proceed to discover the landmarks and impressive monuments of Brussels on a walking orientation tour. Grand Place - Marvel at the medieval majesty of the Market square, gaze at the remarkable facade of the Town hall, Guild houses, Serclaes Monument and the famous Manneken Pis Statue and photo stop at Atomium. Overnight in Rotterdam.
  • Day 6

    Amsterdam/Rotterdam--Cologne/Mannheim


    Today proceed to Madurodam – Holland in miniature. Feel like a giant amidst models of palaces, canals, windmills, harbors, airports and other Holland attractions replicated in minute detail OR visit Keukenhof (from 21 Mar – 12 May) – the largest spring garden in the world. You will be spellbound at the sight of beautiful displays of over seven million Tulips, Daffodils and other flowers, spread over 80 acres of stunning landscape and thematic gardens. Then enjoy a canal cruise, which is the best way to get a good impression of how Amsterdam was built and how much still remains from the Golden Age. Proceed to Mannheim. En-route make a photo-stop at Cologne Cathedral – the largest Gothic church and an iconic symbol of the city as it bore the brunt of 70 airborne bombs during the World War II and yet remained intact towering over the city.
  • Day 7

    Cologne/Mannheim--Titisee--Zurich--Lucerne


    Today we proceed to Titisee, travel through the Black forest region – an evergreen magical land full of cultural traditions and home to cuckoo clocks at Drubba, striking half-timber houses, ruined castles and quaint towns. Visit Cuckoo clock factory at Drubba, Titisee. En-route visit Rhine Falls at Schaffhausen (Neuhausen) enjoy Boat ride for a spectacular view of the falls. Pass by Zurich Lake. Arrive at Lucerne - City situated amid Snow capped mountains, the medieval architecture & wonderful lake makes Lucerne a beautiful & stunning town of Switzerland.
  • Day 8


    Today proceed to Mt Titlis, one of the highest peak in central Switzerland. As you journey to Mt Titlis in two different cable cars, you will be amazed as the scenery changes from green meadows, scenic lakes to snow clad mountains. The last leg of this journey will be in the world's first rotating cable car called the 'Titlis Rotair'. Visit the Ice Grotto and enjoy a ride on the thrilling Ice Flyer ride and Snow sliding (subject to operation). If weather permits, you can take a stroll on the latest attraction at Mt Titlis – The Cliff Walk. Next enjoy an orientation tour of Lucerne where you will see Lion Monument and the Kapellbrucke (wooden bridge). Here you will have some free time to shop for the famous Swiss Watches, knives and chocolates. Later enjoy a scenic cruise on the serene waters of Lake Lucerne.
  • Day 9

    Lucerne--Jungfraujoch


    Begin this day with a visit to Jungfraujoch – The Top of Europe, for a memorable encounter with snow and ice.arrive at Grindelwald terminal to board a fastest & modern attraction "Eiger Express" high speed cable car to reach Eiger Glacier railway station. Enjoy cogwheel train ride, which takes you to a height of 3500 meters to reach Jungfraujoch – Europe's highest railway station! Experience the magic of the mountains and visit the Ice Palace on top of the tallest glacier in the Alps. Take an elevator to the Sphinx observatory deck. Later proceed to Eiger Glacier by cogwheel train to board “Eiger Express” a high speed cable car till Grindelwald Terminal. Later drive through Interlaken city.
  • Day 10

    Lucerne--Vaduz--Wattens--Innsbruck


    Drive through Vaduz – the capital of the principality of Liechtenstein. Enjoy the surroundings of Vaduz in this charming & quite tram ride around the town. Visit Swarovski Crystal World and Museum at Wattens - Sparkling Chambers of Wonder and a fascinating garden with a water-spouting giant that magically lures the beholder into a hidden world. Enjoy a mesmerising experience in the 'world of crystals'. Later make your way to Innsbruck. On arrival enjoy city orientation tour of Innsbruck. See the famous Golden Roof as you stroll on cobblestone pathways of Maria Theresien Strasse and get a feel of old European charm of Innsbruck - Colorful, picturesque city nestled in the Austrian alps.
  • Day 11

    Innsbruck--Venice--Padova


    Today proceed to Venice – the city of romance. Board your Vaporetto water bus to reach the Venice Island. Experience one of the most truly Venetian moments on a Gondola ride. Glide through the canals in your Gondola and see the hidden places only accessible by Gondola. On your orientation tour of Venice see the Doge's Palace, Bell Tower, Bridge of Sighs – which served as a link between the Doge's Palace and the New Prisons, St Marks Square and the Basilica. Later make your way to Padova. Overnight in Padova.
  • Day 12

    Padova--Pisa--Siena--Arezzo


    Today depart to Pisa , on arrival enjoy Pisa city orientation tour which includes Square of Miracles, Baptistry, Cathedral and Leaning tower of Pisa - UNESCO heritage site. Later proceed to Arezzo, en route enjoy Sienna – a Gem of Tuscany, take orientation tour covering Piazza del Campo, Duomo di Siena, Piazza Salimbeni – The oldest bank in the world. Arrive at Arezzo in the evening.
  • Day 13

    Arezzo--Vatican--Rome


    Proceed to the Vatican City – the smallest country in the world. Take a photo-stop at World renowned Vatican Square. Visit the holy basilica of St. Peter’s – famous for The Pieta Sculpture and home of architectural and artistic masterpieces, which will you spellbound.Arrive in Rome. On arrival enjoy City orientation tour see the Roman Forum – an impressive sprawl of ruins which was the political and commercial hub of the Roman Empire. See the Circus Maximus – a Roman chariot racing stadium and mass entertainment venue of ancient Rome followed by the famous Piazza Venezia, a square located in the heart of Rome & Colosseum From Outside. Then take a brief stop at Trevi Fountain – Rome's most famous fountain, known for its legend that states if you throw a coin in the water, you will return to Rome someday..
  • Day 14

    Departure from Rome


    Board the flight for your home town. Tour Concludes.
