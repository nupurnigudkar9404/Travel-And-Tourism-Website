Days Plan

  • Day 1

    Kuala Lumpur


    Arrive at Kuala Lumpur. On arrival city orientation tour which includes Dataran Merdeka Square, National Mosque, Cricket Field, High Court Building.Take a photo stop at King's palace. Visit Suria KLCC which is located at the feet of the Petronas Twin Towers, photo stop at Petronas Twin Towers. Enjoy magnificent view of city from the Observatory Deck of the K. L. Tower.
  • Day 2

    Kuala Lumpur--Genting Highlands


    Proceed to Genting Highlands by coach. Enroute get a chance to Photostop at Batu caves. Travel by Asia's longest and fastest Cable car to Genting Highlands. Visit the New attraction Skytropolis Indoor Theme Park promises endless fun for everyone in the family.Great indoor theme park within the sky avenue mall with different levels of rides suitable for all different ages. Later free time to enjoy at casino and do some shopping. Return back to Kuala Lumpur.
  • Day 3

    Kuala Lumpur--Singapore


    Proceed to Singapore by coach. On arrival visit Gardens by the bay – A Spanning 101 hectares, this super park is home to over a quarter of a million rare plants. Visit Flower Dome and Set within the visually stunning iconic Cloud Forest at Gardens by the Bay, will elevate guest visits with impressive interactives and striking content throughout five different zones and enjoy the sound and light show at The Supertree grove.
  • Day 4

    Singapore--Marina Bay Sands


    Experience the Singapore Flyer ride offering the breathtaking panorama of the shimmering Marina Bay waterfront and historical landmarks dotting the city skyline. Then proceed to visit Southeast Asia's first and only Universal Studio theme park - a fun destination for all ages. Enjoy famous rides like Human vs Cyclon , Transformers - The Ultimate 3D battle, Shrek 4-D Adventure, Light Camera Action and many more fun rides. Later visit Night Safari is the world's first safari park for nocturnal animals. The wildlife park is home to more than 2500 animals from over 130 species at Mandai.
  • Day 5

    Singapore--Merlion


    Proceed for city orientation tour which includes the Parliament House, Suntec City, Fountain of Wealth, City Hall, Orchard Road and Swiss-the tallest hotel in Singapore, the statue of Sir Thomas Stamford Raffles and take a photo stop at Merlion. Free time for shopping. Experience the Cable Car ride from Mt. Faber to Sentosa Island. Visit Madame Tussaud's which displays an array of wax sculptures & other wax works, also enjoy Marvel 4D Cinema – an experience not to be missed. Enjoy the sea breeze and the sun as you venture out traversing along the Singapore waters on the Yacht at Sentosa. Visit S. E. A. Aquarium – the largest oceanarium in the world, which has the world's largest viewing panel and contains 100,000 marine animals of over 800 species. Enjoy the 'Wings of Time' - a spectacular night show set outdoors against the backdrop of an open sea in the evening. Take Sentosa Express (Monorail Ride).
  • Day 6

    Departure from Singapore


    Board the flight to your Hometown. Tour concludes.
