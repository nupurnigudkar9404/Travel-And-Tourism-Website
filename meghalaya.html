Meghalaya: Mawsmai Cave, Elephanta Falls, Balpakram National Park, Shillong, Cherrapunji

Days Plan

  • Day 1

    Shillong


    Arrival in Shillong: Land in Shillong, the capital city of Meghalaya, known as the "Scotland of the East." Check-in: Check into your hotel and freshen up. Ward's Lake: Begin your exploration with a visit to Ward's Lake, a picturesque artificial lake surrounded by gardens and walking paths. Police Bazaar: Explore Police Bazaar, Shillong's bustling market area, known for its vibrant atmosphere and diverse range of shops and eateries.
  • Day 2

    Don Bosco Museum


    Start your day with a visit to Shillong Peak, the highest point in the city, offering panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys. Don Bosco Museum: Explore the Don Bosco Museum, showcasing the rich cultural heritage and traditions of the indigenous tribes of Northeast India through exhibits and artifacts. Cathedral of Mary Help of Christians: Visit the Cathedral of Mary Help of Christians, a beautiful Roman Catholic cathedral known for its architectural beauty and serene ambiance.
  • Day 3

    Cherrapunji Excursion


    Drive to Cherrapunji: Depart for Cherrapunji, renowned as one of the wettest places on Earth and famous for its lush green landscapes and numerous waterfalls. Nohkalikai Falls: Visit Nohkalikai Falls, the tallest plunge waterfall in India, cascading down from a height of over 1,100 feet amidst verdant surroundings. Mawsmai Cave: Explore Mawsmai Cave, a limestone cave known for its intricate formations and natural beauty. Embark on a guided tour through the illuminated passages
  • Day 4

    Dawki and Mawlynnong


    Drive to Dawki: Depart for Dawki, a picturesque town situated on the border between India and Bangladesh, known for its crystal-clear Umngot River. Boating on Umngot River: Enjoy a scenic boat ride on the crystal-clear waters of Umngot River, known for its transparent visibility. Mawlynnong Village: Visit Mawlynnong, often referred to as the "Cleanest Village in Asia." Explore the well-maintained lanes, living root bridges, and panoramic views from the Sky Walk viewpoint.
  • Day 5

    Nongriat Trek


    Trek to Nongriat: Embark on a trek to Nongriat village, famous for its living root bridges. Begin the trek from Tyrna village and descend through lush forests and picturesque landscapes. Double Decker Living Root Bridge: Reach Nongriat and marvel at the awe-inspiring Double Decker Living Root Bridge, a natural wonder formed by the intricate root system of ancient trees. Leisure Time: Spend the evening relaxing in the serene surroundings of Nongriat village, listening to the sounds of nature and soaking in the tranquility.
  • Day 5

    David Scott Trait


    avid Scott Trail: Embark on a trek along the David Scott Trail, an ancient trail named after a British administrator, offering scenic views of the surrounding hills and valleys.
  • Day 6

    Mawphlang Grove


    Mawphlang Sacred Grove: Visit Mawphlang Sacred Grove, a protected forest area revered by the local Khasi tribe for its spiritual significance and biodiversity. David Scott Trail: Embark on a trek along the David Scott Trail, an ancient trail named after a British administrator, offering scenic views of the surrounding hills and valleys. Local Cultural Experience: Experience traditional Khasi culture with a visit to a local village, where you can interact with villagers, learn about their customs, and enjoy a traditional meal.
  • Day 7

    Departure from Shillong


    Check out from your hotel and proceed to Shillong Airport for your onward journey, bidding farewell to the enchanting landscapes of Meghalaya with cherished memories.
