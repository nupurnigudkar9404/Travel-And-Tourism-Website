Days Plan

  • Day 1

    Arrival in Kochi


    Arrive at Kochi International Airport or Ernakulam Railway Station. Check-in at your hotel and freshen up. Fort Kochi: Explore the historic Fort Kochi area, known for its colonial architecture, narrow streets, and cultural heritage. Visit landmarks like the iconic Chinese Fishing Nets, St. Francis Church, and the Mattancherry Palace (Dutch Palace). Lunch: Enjoy traditional Kerala cuisine at a local restaurant in Fort Kochi.
  • Day 2

    Backwater Experience in Alleppey


    Depart for Alleppey, known as the "Venice of the East" for its scenic backwaters. Houseboat Cruise: Embark on a houseboat cruise through the tranquil backwaters of Alleppey. Enjoy breathtaking views of lush greenery, palm-fringed landscapes, and traditional village life. Lunch on Houseboat: Indulge in a delicious Kerala-style lunch prepared by the onboard chef as you cruise along the backwaters. Backwater Retreat: Spend the evening relaxing on the houseboat, enjoying the serene ambiance and beautiful sunset views over the backwaters.
  • Day 3

    Hill Station Retreat in Munnar


    Depart for Munnar, a picturesque hill station renowned for its tea plantations, misty mountains, and lush valleys. Tea Plantation Visit: Stop at a tea plantation en route to Munnar and learn about the tea-making process during a guided tour.Enjoy a meal at a local restaurant in Munnar, offering panoramic views of the surrounding hills. Relaxation: Check-in at your hotel in Munnar and unwind amidst the serene surroundings of the hill station. Take a leisurely walk or simply relax and enjoy the cool mountain breeze.
  • Day 4

    Explore Munnar's Natural Beauty


    Visit Eravikulam National Park, home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr and lush Shola forests. Take a guided safari to explore the park's biodiversity and stunning landscapes. Mattupetty Dam: Head to Mattupetty Dam, nestled amidst rolling hills and tea gardens. Enjoy boating on the reservoir or go for a nature walk around the area. Echo Point: Visit Echo Point, where you can shout out and hear your voice echo across the hills. Enjoy the scenic views and capture memorable moments amidst nature.
  • Day 5

    Beach Retreat in Kovalam


    Depart for Kovalam, a popular beach destination known for its golden sands and clear blue waters. Lighthouse Beach: Spend your morning relaxing at Lighthouse Beach, the most famous beach in Kovalam. Enjoy sunbathing, swimming, or water sports activities. Lunch: Savor fresh seafood and other delicacies at a beachside restaurant overlooking the Arabian Sea. Relaxation: Check-in at your beachside resort and unwind with a rejuvenating Ayurvedic massage or yoga session by the sea.
  • Day 5

    Explore Trivandrum and Padmanabhaswamy Temple


    Visit the historic Padmanabhaswamy Temple in Trivandrum, dedicated to Lord Vishnu. Explore the intricate Dravidian architecture and marvel at the temple's grandeur. Napier Museum: Explore the Napier Museum, located adjacent to the Padmanabhaswamy Temple. Discover a fascinating collection of ancient artifacts, bronze sculptures, and traditional Kerala art. Shangumugham Beach: Head to Shangumugham Beach to catch the sunset and enjoy a peaceful evening by the sea. Take a leisurely stroll along the sandy shore and watch local fishermen at work.
  • Day 6

    Padmanabhaswamy Temple


    Visit the historic Padmanabhaswamy Temple in Trivandrum, dedicated to Lord Vishnu. Explore the intricate Dravidian architecture and marvel at the temple's grandeur. Napier Museum: Explore the Napier Museum, located adjacent to the Padmanabhaswamy Temple. Discover a fascinating collection of ancient artifacts, bronze sculptures, and traditional Kerala art.
  • Day 7

    Sunrise at Kanyakumari and Departure


    Depart early in the morning for Kanyakumari, the southernmost tip of the Indian peninsula. Witness a breathtaking sunrise over the confluence of the Arabian Sea, Bay of Bengal, and Indian Ocean. Vivekananda Rock Memorial: Visit the Vivekananda Rock Memorial, located on a rocky island off the coast of Kanyakumari. Explore the memorial dedicated to Swami Vivekananda and meditate at the meditation hall. Thiruvalluvar Statue: See the Thiruvalluvar Statue, a massive sculpture honoring the ancient Tamil poet and philosopher, Thiruvalluvar. Admire the statue's grandeur and panoramic views of the sea. Departure: After exploring Kanyakumari, proceed to Trivandrum International Airport or the railway station for your onward journey.
