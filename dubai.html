Days Plan

  • Day 1

    Dubai


    Arrive in Dubai - one of the world's most popular tourist destination. In 18th century it was a small fishing village, which has now transformed into one of the wealthiest cosmopolitan city.
  • Day 2

    Dubai


    Today morning we proceed to enjoy city orientation tour, which includes Sheikh Zayed Road, Golf Club and pass by Atlantis and Dubai marina area. Take photo stop at Atlantis Hotel and Museum of The Future. Later enjoy the The View at the Palm to get 360-degree views of Palm Jumeirah, the Arabian Gulf and beyond Spot iconic landmarks and hidden gems of this beautiful city. Drive through Palm Islands and take a quick photo break. Later enjoy shopping at Deira City centre. In the evening enjoy Marina Dhow Dinner Cruise – a traditional wooden sailing vessel, relax and enjoy spectacular views of modern architecture and the fascinating old city. Later enjoy the luxurious chauffeur driven Limousine ride.
  • Day 3

    Dubai--Gold Souk--Desert Safari


    Visit Miracle Garden - one of the most beautiful and biggest natural flower gardens in the world, featuring over 45 million flowers and plants. Later enjoy shopping at Gold Souk. In the evening experience the thrill of the most popular Desert Dune Safari by a Four Wheel drive. Enjoy the exciting Belly Dance show with dinner.
  • Day 4

    Museum of Future--Burj Khalifa


    Today explore the Museum Of The Future (subject to availability) - one of the most beautiful building on earth. Explore this multisensorial architectural and engineering marvel, which acts as a cross-bridge between the past and the future using advanced technology. Visit different sections like digital Amazon, DNA Library, Today we visit the 124th floor of Burj Khalifa via the high-speed elevators and enjoy the 360-degree views from 1483 feet. Here we also enjoy the fountain show at Burj Khalifa (If Time Permits). Then we also visit AYA Dubai - A beyond the world experience, explore the luminous gardens, comand galaxies and dance with light. It is a sanctuary hidden beyond the far of the star.
  • Day 5

    Sea World--Abu Dhabi


    Morning we proceed to Abu Dhabi. On arrival we visit to Sea World - With over 100 animal experiences, it is home to over 35 interactive experiences and rides, live characters and performances.
  • Day 6

    Abu Dhabi


    Morning enjoy city orientation tour of Abu Dhabi and visit Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque – this architectural work of art is one of the world’s largest mosques, with a capacity for an astonishing 41,000 worshipers. Then we proceed to visit BAPS Hindu Mandir in Abu Dhabi - the first traditional stone temple and the largest Hindu temple in West Asia. Later explore the massive indoor theme of Warner bros. and get transported to awesome worlds of action and adventure, whimsy and wackiness straight out of your favourite cartoons and movies!
  • Day 7

    Ferrari World--Departure


    Visit Ferrari Park - the largest indoor theme park on the planet and the first Ferrari-branded theme park in the world. Board the flight for your home town. Tour concludes.
